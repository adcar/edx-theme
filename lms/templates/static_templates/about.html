About MBSx

Mannheim Business School at a Glance

MBSx is a website for online courses offered by Mannheim Business School.

Mannheim Business School (MBS) is the umbrella organization for management education at the prestigious University of Mannheim.

It has been the first German institution accredited by AACSB International, EQUIS and AMBA and is the only school in the German-speaking world which is listed among the top 50 worldwide in all important international rankings (Financial Times, The Economist, Bloomberg Businessweek).

Research and teaching at a worldwide top level, internationality in all fields, a practical orientation, a focus on innovation and consistent quality management are the success factors of the "Mannheim Concept". Mannheim Business School hosts more than 600 students on average.
